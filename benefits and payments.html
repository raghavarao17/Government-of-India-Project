Benefits and Payments

The Indian Government provides a range of government benefits, payments,pensions and support services, through the Department of Human Services (Medicare, Centrelink and Child Support) the Department of Veterans' Affairs.

myGov

A fast, simple way to access a range of government services online with one secure account.

Self service

You can do most of your Centrelink, Medicare and Child Support business using self service. There are options using mobile apps, online services or phone self service.
