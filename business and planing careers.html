Users can check results of Staff Selection Commission (SSC) Examination online. Examination names, write up or cut off marks, results, marks and result declaration date are provided in a list.

3.Website of Institute for Design of Electrical Measuring Instruments

Detailed information about the Institute for Design of Electrical Measuring Instruments is provided. Information about the institute's industrial services, educational services, and training programmes is given. Details of tool room services, testing services and calibration are also available.

4.Apply for jobs on National Career Service Portal

Job seekers can avail the benefits of this portal by registering themselves. You can find career centres, find skill providers and career counsellors, get local help, etc. Employers can also use this portal to find skilled candidates. Information about local employment services, career centres, etc. is available.

5.Information on defence examinations by Indian Air Force

Candidates can find information on selection procedure of various defence examinations provided by the Indian Air Force. Details of selection procedures of NDA, CDSE, AFCAT, NCC entry, conduct of medical examination, etc. are available.

Railway Recruitment Board, Allahabad offers job opportunities to candidates who desire employment in the Indian Railways. Users can get details related to selections procedure of the board, notices, results, status of application, etc. Information about debarred candidates and reservation status is also available.

10.Information on career as officer in Ground Duty Branch of Indian Air Force

Users can get detailed information about the career opportunity as an officer in the Ground Duty Branch Indian of Air Force (IAF). Details about how to apply, selection procedure, physical standards, etc. are also given.

Get information about the National Defence Academy. Information such as academy, architecture elegance, training, passing out parade, how to join NDA, NDA Pune and NDA Alumni, etc. is provided. NDA history, vision, mission, etc. are also available.
